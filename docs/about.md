**Research**

I did my bachelor's and master's studies in statistics at University of Helsinki, Finland. In 2022, I started working on a PhD in Electrical Engineering at Aalto University, graduating in late 2025. Most of my research has focused on developing sequential change-point detection methods that are able deal with the challenges and opportunities of modern-day problems: in partcular high dimensions, multiple comparison/testing issues, and uncertainty regarding probability models. Other stats/machine learning topics I find interesting and would like to work more on include conformal inference, game-theoretic probability/statistics and causal inference.
